Learn To Make Cold Coffee!!

"When life gives you lemons , trade them for iced coffee."

Today lets begin with the recipe of cold coffee....

Ingredients

All you need is

S.no Ingredient Quantity
1 Instant coffee powder 2 1/2 teaspoons
2 Chilled milk 2 cup
3 Sugar 1 tablespoon(adjust per taste)
4 ice cubes 6

Preparation

Step 1-Take a cup, add two teaspoons of coffee powder, some sugar & two tablespoons of milk.

Step 2- Now with the help of the spoon, beat the mixture vigorously until the color changes & starts forming froth.

Step 3- Keep repeating the above step until 5-7 mins and then add the remaining chilled milk into the foamed coffee & stir properly.

Step 4- Pour the coffee in your favorite cup & add ice cubes from the top. The easy to make cold coffee is ready.
